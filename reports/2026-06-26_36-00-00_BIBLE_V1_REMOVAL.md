# Bible V1 Removal — Canonical V2 at `/bible` & `/books`

**Date:** 2026-06-26  
**Scope:** Remove duplicate old Bible hub; unify on Bible V2 experience.

---

## Executive Summary

The legacy Bible home (`BibleHomeScreen` at `/bible/`) and old books grid (`/books` inline grid) were retired. The canonical routes are now **`/bible`** (Bible V2) and **`/books`** (Books V2). Legacy preview URLs **`/bible-2`** and **`/books-v2`** redirect permanently. The duplicate home card «الكتاب المقدس 2» was removed. Build **PASS**.
